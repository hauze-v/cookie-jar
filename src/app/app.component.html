The Cookie Jar Method

Made famous by David Goggins, the cookie jar method helps you overcome self-doubt by identifying past acomplishments (cookies) and gathering them into a container (cookie jar) you draw from when you're feeling low.

How It Works

Sign up to gain access to your own cookie jar. From there, you can add, edit, and delete accomplishments (cookies) and smack the big cookie jar to randomly display one for you when you're feeling down!.
